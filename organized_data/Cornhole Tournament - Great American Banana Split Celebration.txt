0	Cornhole Tournament See what's happening
1	 Tournament Information Grab your best throwing partner to compete in the annual Cornhole Tournament! Prizes will be awarded to the winners
2	 Register HERE Details Saturday, August 24, 2024 12pm to 5pm 329 Main Street Parking Lot (Over 21 Area) Rules GAME PLAY  Two teams of two people, termed partners, shall play
3	 Partners shall stand at opposite cornhole boxes on the same side, from the perspective of a third person, and face each other, so there is no advantage given to one team
4	  Each cornhole team shall have 4 bags of one color
5	 All 8 cornhole bags begin at one end
6	  A coin flip shall determine which cornhole team has honors in round one
7	  One of the partners on the team who has honors shall begin play by throwing a cornhole bag at the opposite cornhole box
8	 A cornhole player may throw from anywhere behind the front of the cornhole box they are throwing from
9	 Game continues by alternating throws between the two opponent players until all 8 cornhole bags have been thrown
10	  If a cornhole bag hits the ground then bounces up onto the board, that bag shall be taken off the playing surface
11	  The next round starts when the other cornhole player on the team, which has honors, throws their first cornhole bag
12	  The cornhole game continues until one team reaches 21 or more points
13	 SCORING  3 points for a cornhole bag that goes through the hole
14	  1 point for a cornhole bag that is on the playing surface
15	  1 point for a cornhole bag that is hanging into the hole
16	  1 point for a cornhole bag that is hanging off the edge but not touching the ground
17	  0 points for a cornhole bag that is on the playing surface, but also touching the ground
18	  0 points for a cornhole bag that is hanging off the front edge and is resting on a cornhole bag that is on the ground (unless the cornhole bag on the ground can be removed without making the hanging bag fall to the ground, then one point is given)
19	  The cornhole team with the highest round score, adds the difference of the two scores to their game score
20	  The cornhole team who wins the round is given honors to throw first in the next round
21	  If both cornhole teams have the same round score then the game score stays the same and honors stays with the team who had it the previous round
