Picklesburgh is the destination for all things pickled! Voted the #1 Specialty Food Festival in the Country for three consecutive years by the readers of USA Today, Picklesburgh goes beyond the dill pickle to feature handcrafted food and artisan drinks featuring pickled ingredients from local chefs, restaurants, and more embracing the farm-to-table movement
1	 Plus, don’t miss pickle-themed snacks and merchandise, fun contests, and live music set to the backdrop of picturesque Downtown Pittsburgh
2	 When is it? Picklesburgh will return in July 2025
3	 Dates & hours for the 2025 Festival to be announced
4	 Stay tuned! Where is it? Picklesburgh is an annual festival that takes place in Downtown Pittsburgh
5	 How much does it cost? Admission is always free – no tickets are necessary! You must be 21+ to consume/purchase alcohol and proper ID will be required
6	 Plentiful Picklesburgh Parking ! No matter where you’re coming from there are lots of convenient parking options for Picklesburgh
7	 Park near Picklesburgh or across town to avoid Festival traffic
8	 Real-time parking availability all around Downtown: https://parkpgh
