1	 The Italian American Collection is one of the largest repositories of Italian American artifacts, photographs, oral histories, and archival materials in the United States
2	 For over two decades, it has documented the pivotal role Italian Americans play in shaping the region’s political, economic, religious, and cultural landscapes

12	 The Italian American Advisory Council is made up of scholars, educators, executives, entrepreneurs, politicians, and community leaders
