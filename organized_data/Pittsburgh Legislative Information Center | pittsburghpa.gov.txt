LAUNCH City Council takes pride in its open form of government and is pleased to provide state-of-the-art technology to assist the public in tracking and retrieving legislation
3	 Below you will find three tabs: Legislation , Video Streaming , and Meeting Calendar 
4	 Please click on each tab and view some information we've assembled about using the LIC
5	 Note: Official, certified copies can be obtained in person from the City Clerk's Office
6	 Searching for Legislation In the LIC, the Legislation page is a common starting point for users
7	 You may search the legislation database by keyword, by year, by type, and more by means of an advanced search interface (you will see the Advanced Search link to the right of the search controls)
8	 More detailed help with searching is available here 
9	 After you initiate a search, your results are returned in rows below the search interface
10	 Legislative File numbers are hyperlinked to Detail pages 
11	 Detail pages A Legislative Detail page contains a wealth of information about a specific piece of legislation - sponsors, titles, committee of control, etc
12	 Any attachments to the legislation are available here
13	 The Legislative Detail page is where you can track Actions taken as the file moves through the legislative process
14	 You can see how votes were cast by clicking Action Details 
15	 All details of the meeting are linked to - click Meeting Details 
16	 After meetings take place, the City Clerk's office works hard to edit and archive Meeting Video
17	 When the video clip has been archived, you will find a link on the Legislative Detail page
18	 For more help with video, please go to the Video Streaming tab above
19	 QUICK HELP & HINTS When searching for legislation, make sure to check what year you are searching
20	 The search control defaults to the current year
21	 You may track specific legislation and receive updates via RSS
22	 Look for the RSS icon: ATTACHMENT FILES Legislative attachments are posted in
