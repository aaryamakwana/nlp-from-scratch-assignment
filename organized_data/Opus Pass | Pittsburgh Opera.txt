22	 FAQS - PRICING/BILLING How much does an Opus Pass cost? The Pittsburgh Opera Opus Pass costs $20 a month
23	 Subscribers can add an Opus Pass Subscription Add On to their traditional seated subscription package for an extra $5 a month
24	 For more information about the Opus Pass Subscription Add On, please see the 'FAQs - Opus Pass Subscription Add On' section below
25	 How do I buy an Opus Pass? You can buy a Pittsburgh Opera Opus Pass by calling 412-281-0912 ext
26	 3, or you can order online 
27	 How long does my Opus Pass last? Pittsburgh Opera Opus Passes are valid for one year from its date of issue
28	 Your Opus Pass will automatically renew unless you take action to make changes or cancel
29	 When does my Opus Pass 'start'? If you purchase your Opus Pass between June-September 2024, it will activate as of October 1st, in time for the first opera of our 2024-25 season
30	 Your billing cycle will begin in early October
31	 If you purchase your Opus Pass on or after October 1, 2024, it is valid for one year from the date of purchase
32	 What if I buy an Opus Pass after the season is already underway? Since your Pittsburgh Opera Opus Passes is valid for one year from its date of issue, you will be able to use your Pass for any Pittsburgh Opera performances in the following season that fall within one year of purchase
33	 For example, if you purchase a Pass in December 2024, you will be able to use it for all our remaining 2024-25 Season performances, as well as our October and November 2024 performances, even though they are part of our 2025-26 season
34	 How do you bill me? Your credit card will be charged once each calendar month
35	 Patrons who buy an Opus Pass will be charged monthly from date of purchase
36	 Can I pay for the whole year upfront? Yes
37	 If you choose our one-time payment option, you will save $20, i
38	e
39	 you will get one month free
40	 My credit card's expiration date is before my Opus Pass expires
41	 What should I do? Please call us at 412-281-0912 ext
42	 3 with your updated credit card information prior to your card expiring
43	 Is it possible to cancel my Opus Pass before it expires? If you need to cancel your Opus Pass before it expires, you can choose to receive a refund for the difference in value between what you've paid so far for your Pass and the normal single ticket face value price of any tickets you redeemed
44	 For example, if you had been a Passholder for six months and paid $120 for your Pass so far, and you'd redeemed your Pass for one ticket which had a regular price of $110, you would be eligible for a $10 refund
45	 If you have redeemed your Pass for tickets whose normal face values totals more than you've paid so far for your Pass, then Pittsburgh Opera reserves the right to charge you for the difference
46	 For example, if you buy a Pass, immediately redeem it for a ticket, and then after the performance you cancel your Pass, we will charge you the difference between the regular ticket price and the $20 you paid for your Pass
47	 The Opus Pass is a great value, we just want to make sure folks don't try to game the system - not that you would ever do that of course! All future ticket reservations will be forfeited upon cancellation of membership
48	 There are no refunds retroactively after your Pass expires

73	 FAQS - GENERAL QUESTIONS Can I give my Opus Pass to someone else when I'm not using it? No
74	 Pittsburgh Opera Opus Passes are non-transferable
75	 You must show Photo ID when picking up your tickets
76	 I normally buy two tickets to the opera - one for me and one for my partner or friend
77	 Do we each need our own Opus Pass if we want to go to the same performances together? It depends
78	 If you and your partner always attend the opera together, i
79	e
80	 you always buy two seats together, and you plan on attending at least three performances this coming season together, then you should buy two Opus Passes - one for you and one for your partner
81	 Households with more than one Opus Pass will need to assign a name to each Pass
82	 If your "+1" guest changes from show to show - sometimes your significant other comes with you, but other times you bring a neighbor, friend, or work colleague - you should not buy two Opus Passes, because each Pass is issued in a specific person's name

98	 FAQS - OPUS PASS SUBSCRIPTION ADD ON I am a Pittsburgh Opera subscriber who wants to keep my seats
99	 Can I get an Opus Pass ? Yes! You can get our Opus Pass Subscription Add On for just an extra $5 a month
100	 Subscribers who elect the Add On will keep their existing seats for their primary performance, but aren’t guaranteed those same seats for the performances seen with their Opus Pass
101	 The Opus Pass Subscription Add On is a great way to see your favorites more than once and to check out different locations in the theater for a very low price
102	 Can I get the Opus Pass Subscription Add On if I modify my subscription? If you maintain your 2023-24 subscription package for 2024-25, or if you add an extra opera to your package or upgrade your seating zone, you can add the Opus Pass Subscription Add On
103	 But if you 'downgrade', for example go from a 2023-24 Quartet to a 2024-25 Duet package, you cannot add the Opus Pass Subscription Add On
104	 Do I pay for my Opus Pass Add On upfront, or am I billed monthly? Subscribers who select a Opus Pass Add On will have the $60 annual cost for their Pass added to their order total, which can be paid all at once or in installments
105	 Can I still get an Opus Pass Add On if I subscribe after the 2024-25 season is underway? Yes
106	 Your Opus Pass Add On would be valid for the remainder of the 2024-25 season
