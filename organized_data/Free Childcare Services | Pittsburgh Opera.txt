14	 FAQS Where is the childcare provided? The childcare will take place onsite at the Benedum Center, in its multipurpose room on the second floor, which will be used exclusively for this service
15	 What are the age limits? The minimum age to attend these opera performances and sit in the audience is six
16	 However, children as young as two will be able to use the childcare service 
17	 Children between the ages of six and twelve have the option of seeing the opera with tickets at 50% off or using the childcare service at no charge
18	 Do I need to buy a ticket for my child who will be using the childcare service? Yes, but it's free
19	 Any child using the childcare service must register in advance 
20	 Please use the process shown in the "How to add Childcare to your ticket purchase" section below to add Childcare tickets to your shopping cart while you are purchasing your tickets to see the opera performance
21	 You will receive a 'ticket' for the childcare which the usher will scan when you enter the Benedum just like they'll scan your ticket to the performance
22	 Once I order my childcare ticket, am I done? Not quite
23	 Your order confirmation email will include a link to an online registration form to fill out
24	 This form will ask for information about things such as your seating location, the child's name and age, any allergies the children have, and other such matters
25	 You will need to fill out and submit that form in advance
26	 The form includes a liability waiver, which you can read in the "Liability Waiver" section of this webpage
27	 The parent/guardian must reconfirm the registration information at drop off, and remain in the building during the performance 
28	 When do I drop off and pick up my child? The Benedum Center opens 1 hour 15 minutes before the performance starts
29	 You may drop your child off anytime after the Benedum opens up until the beginning of the performance
30	 You are welcome to visit your child during intermission if you wish
31	 Children must be picked up within 10 minutes of the performance’s ending
32	 Children may only be released to the person that signed them in to the childcare area
33	 NO EXCEPTIONS
34	 After I drop my kid off, can I duck across the street for a bite to eat before the show starts? No! In fact, double No
35	 The parent/guardian must remain in the building during the performance
36	 Can I bring toys or books or games, or their favorite stuffed animal, for my child to use? Yes, provided you adhere to the Pittsburgh Cultural Trust's Bag Policy and their Prohibited Items policy 
37	 Pittsburgh Opera will have a range of age-appropriate activities available for your child to use
38	 Will there be food provided? Parents may feed their children snacks, e
39	g
40	 during intermission, but the childcare providers will not provide food to the children
41	 Please note that parents must follow the Pittsburgh Cultural Trusts' outside food and beverage policy 
42	 What about bathrooms and dirty diapers? Childcare staff will cover diaper changes and/or accompany kids to the bathroom unless the parent requests otherwise
43	 Parents must bring their child's diapers/wipes/pullups
44	 My child takes medication every afternoon
45	 Will you give my child their regular daily medication? No, medications will not be administered by childcare staff
