4	 Pittsburgh Opera’s Resident Artist Program has long been hailed as one of the country's leading training programs for emerging opera artists and administrators
5	 While providing guidance from the opera industry’s leaders and innovators, the program guides singers through the development of their vocal skills, improvement of their stage presence, and expansion of their repertoire
6	 Each year, exceptional singers from around the world are selected to enter the program through live auditions in Cincinnati, New York City, and Pittsburgh, and since 2020, through a virtual audition option
7	 Each applicant must demonstrate significant operatic potential and thorough musical and theatrical training
8	 Applications for Pittsburgh Opera’s Resident Artist Program will be available from August 6 – September 3, 2024 
9	 To apply, complete our online application via YAP Tracker at the link below: 2025-27 Resident Artist Program: Singer Application (yaptracker
10	com) REMINDER: The deadline to apply is Tuesday, September 3, 2024 
11	 You do not need to be a paying subscriber to YAP Tracker to use the application form, although you will need an account (guest accounts are available; see the website for details)
12	 If you need help completing the application or using YAP Tracker, please visit https://www
13	yaptracker
14	com/more/help/getting-started/ or email their help desk directly at help@yaptracker

17	 Two (2) video recordings of selections in contrasting styles and languages recorded within the last six months
18	 One must be an opera aria; one may be a performance recording, if desired
19	 Recordings may be made with either live accompaniment or a pre-recorded track
20	 Your audition repertoire list

27	 A current headshot
28	 There is no fee to apply for Pittsburgh Opera's Resident Artist Program

30	 In addition, we will again offer video auditions
31	 We encourage live auditions whenever possible, but welcome those who are unavailable or unable to sing in-person at one of our audition locations to apply for a video audition

44	 RESIDENT ARTIST FAQ Download a PDF of this FAQ here
45	 Who is on the audition panel? Christopher Hahn, General Director; Robert Boldin, Director of Artistic Operations; Mark Trawka, Director of Musical Studies
46	 Is there an age limit to participate in Pittsburgh Opera’s Resident Artist Program? There is not an official age limit, but most of our Resident Artists are in their 20’s and early 30’s
47	 Singers are more likely to be granted an audition if they are about to or have recently completed formal academic training, but this is not a requirement to be considered for the program
48	 Do I need to be a U
49	S
50	 citizen to attend the program? Pittsburgh Opera will not apply for a visa on your behalf
51	 If you have a current visa that will allow you to work in the United States for the full two-year program, we encourage you to apply
52	 What do I need to record? Do you have any guidance on how to record my selections? Applicants are required to submit two (2) video recordings of selections in contrasting styles and languages recording within the last six months
53	 One must be an opera aria; one may be a performance recording, if desired
54	 Recordings may be made with either live accompaniment or a pre-recorded track
55	 For Opera America's resources on Remote Auditions please visit: Opera America - 2020 Remote Audition Guidelines Opera America - Audio Gear Recommendations When will I find out if I have been selected for a live or virtual audition? Notifications for both live and virtual auditions will be sent on Friday, October 18, 2024
56	 If I’m granted a virtual audition, what do I need to record? Applicants who are granted a virtual audition will be required to record and submit two selections in one continuous video recording (without stopping the camera)
57	 This video MUST be recorded on or after September 4, 2024
58	 Repertoire for video auditions will be selected by the singer
59	 We strongly recommend submitting contrasting selections, and preferably ones that you did not submit with your original application
60	 Guidelines for repertoire selection and video production will be provided
61	 The deadline for submitting a virtual audition video is November 15, 2024 
62	 What if I need to change my live audition date/time? First, you should try to find a colleague who is willing to switch with you
63	 Once you have made the switch, e-mail Matthew Hrdlicka, Artistic Coordinator, to notify him of the change
64	 If you cannot make the switch yourself, please contact Matthew Hrdlicka and best efforts will be made to accommodate your request
65	 If I need to cancel my live audition, can I submit virtual audition materials instead? If you need to cancel your live audition but would like to be considered virtually, please contact Matthew Hrdlicka , Artistic Coordinator
66	 How many arias will I sing? If you are selected for a live audition, you will sing one aria of your choice
67	 If you are selected for a call back audition, you will sing two additional arias – one of your choice, followed by one aria of the panel’s choice
68	 If you are selected for a virtual audition, you will sing two arias of your choice
69	 What if I don’t know an aria in every language requested? You should sing the arias you are most comfortable performing
70	 What if I want to change my audition repertoire? If you are selected for a live audition, you may update your repertoire at any time via YAP Tracker or submit a new repertoire list at the time of your audition
71	 Virtual applicants selected for the second round will be required to re-submit a repertoire list for consideration by the panel
72	 What materials do I need to bring for the audition panel? The panel will have all materials submitted with your application
73	 If you have an updated repertoire list or resume, you may submit it to the monitor at the time of your audition
74	 Is there a place to warm-up before my audition? Warm-up space is limited and not guaranteed depending on the location
75	 Please come to the audition prepared to sing
76	 How do I find out about live callback auditions? If granted a callback audition, you will be notified via YAP Tracker with the details
77	 When are casting decisions made? Decisions will be made following the New York callback auditions
78	 Offers will be given by telephone sometime early in the new year
79	 Will I receive feedback from the audition panel? We regret that panelists will not be able to offer feedback immediately following the auditions or at any time thereafter
