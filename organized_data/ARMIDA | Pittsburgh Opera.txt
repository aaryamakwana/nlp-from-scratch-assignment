11	 The bravest of these, Rinaldo, has fallen under Armida's spell
12	 She comes to love him so deeply that she cannot bring herself to destroy him
13	 Act 1 Scene 1: A council chamber in the royal palace of Damascus
14	 King Idreno is alarmed that the crusaders have crossed the Jordan River
15	 The heathen sorceress Armida seems to have triumphed over the crusaders, but fears that her conquest is not complete without gaining the love of the Christian knight Rinaldo
16	 Now Rinaldo is obsessed with Armida and promises to fight against his fellow Christians, if victorious King Idreno offers him the kingdom and Armida’s hand
17	 Armida prays for Rinaldo’s safety
18	 Scene 2: A steep mountain, with Armida's fortress at the top
19	 The knights Ubaldo and Clotarco plan to free Rinaldo from Armida’s clutches
20	 Idreno sends Zelmira, the daughter of the sultan of Egypt, to ensnare the Christians but on encountering Clotarco she falls in love with him and offers to lead him to safety
21	 Scene 3: Armida's apartments
22	 Rinaldo admires the bravery of the approaching knights
23	 Ubaldo warns Rinaldo to beware Armida's charms, and reproaches the dereliction of his duty as a Christian
24	 Although remorseful, Rinaldo is unable to escape Armida's enchantment
25	 Act 2 Scene 1: A garden in Armida's palace
26	 Zelmira fails to dissuade Idreno from planning an ambush of the crusaders
27	 Idreno pretends to agree to Clotarco's demand that the Christian knights enchanted by Armida be freed
28	 Reluctantly, Rinaldo leaves with Ubaldo
29	 Armida expresses her fury
30	 Scene 2: The crusader camp
31	 Ubaldo welcomes Rinaldo, who prepares to go into battle
32	 Armida begs for refuge and Rinaldo’s love
33	 Rinaldo departs for battle with Ubaldo and the other soldiers
34	 Act 3 Scene 1: A dark, forbidding grove, with a large myrtle tree
35	 Rinaldo, knowing that the tree holds the secret of Armida’s powers, enters the wood intending to cut it down
36	 Zelmira appears with a group of nymphs, and they try to get him to return to Armida
37	 As he is about to strike the myrtle, Armida, dishevelled, appears from it and confronts him
38	 Armida cannot bring herself to kill him; Rinaldo strikes the tree and the magic wood vanishes
39	 Scene 2: The crusader camp
40	 The crusaders prepare for battle against the Saracens
41	 Armida appears, swearing to pursue Rinaldo everywhere
42	 As Rinaldo moves off, she sends an infernal chariot after Rinaldo
