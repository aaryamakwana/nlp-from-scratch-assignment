9	 Pittsburgh Opera Matching Information: Address: 2425 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh PA 15222 Phone: 412-281-0912 Email: Torey Gricks Tax-ID or EIN #: 25-1073139 WHAT IS A MATCHING GIFT? Employee matching gift programs are corporate giving programs
10	 For example, if you work for Highmark and donate $100 to Pittsburgh Opera, Highmark’s matching gift program has a minimum gift requirement of $100 and matches your gift at a 1:1 ratio
11	 After you submit your matching gift form, Highmark will effectively double your gift by giving $100 to Pittsburgh Opera, as well
12	 Retirees may also qualify for a match
13	 It's a quick and easy way to increase the impact of your gift and leverage your company’s support for Pittsburgh Opera
14	 HOW DO I REQUEST A MATCHING GIFT FROM MY COMPANY TO THE OPERA? Requesting a matching gift is a quick process that you must initiate
15	 You can do this by completing and submitting either a paper form provided by your employer or submitting the information through your company’s electronic submission process
16	 Your company will then send its matching gift to Pittsburgh Opera
17	 There are typically 3 steps: 1
18	 Give to Pittsburgh Opera Make your gift to Pittsburgh Opera and save your tax receipt
19	 Your company’s matching gift program may allow up to one year after you've made your gift for you to request a corporate match
20	 2
21	 Search for your company’s matching gift program Determine if your company has a matching gift program and review the guidelines and your eligibility on your company’s website or contact your company’s human resource department directly to ask whether they offer a matching gift program
22	 3
23	 Submit your Match There are two ways to submit the matching gift request: Click on your company's portal link provided in the search results
24	 Log-in and submit your request electronically
25	 Download your company's matching gift verification form using the link provided in the search results
26	 Print it, fill it out, and send it to Pittsburgh Opera
27	 Pittsburgh Opera 2425 Liberty Ave, Pittsburgh PA 15222 Attn: Torey Gricks WHAT ARE VOLUNTEER GRANT PROGRAMS? Volunteer grant programs are corporate giving programs in which companies provide monetary gifts to organizations where employees volunteer regularly For example, if you volunteer as an usher for our performances, your employer could potentially make a monetary gift for our volunteer time
