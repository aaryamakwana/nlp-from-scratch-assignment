23	org/facilities/hold-your-event-at-pittsburgh-opera/rental-information Load-in & Load-out Policy A full-day rental includes 12 hours from set-up to end of your event, plus one hour for clean-up
24	 Access will be given to Social Tables for lessees to place objects around the space
25	 Pittsburgh Opera event staff will set up the items as noted on Social Tables prior to lessee’s arrival
26	 Lessees will inform Pittsburgh Opera staff of any rentals associated with their event
27	 Drop off and pick up times must be coordinated prior to the event
28	 Vendor drop offs are allowed the day prior to your event within the hours of 9:30am-1:30pm
29	 Lessee will be charged an additional hourly fee for a ceremony rehearsal or drop off and set up of personal items, décor, etc
30	 on the day prior to the event
31	 It is preferred that all personal items must be removed from the building at the end of the event, however, if prior arrangements are made, items may be picked up at a later date
32	 Rented items may be left and picked up on the next business day between 9:30am-1:30pm, with approval from Pittsburgh Opera
33	 Additional Security The staffing fee for each event covers one (1) security guard, hired by PO, for the duration of your Event Time and one (1) PO staff member
34	 Upper Level of GRW Access Guests may NOT access the upper level of the GRW
35	 We will allow individuals up to the second level to put up and take down decorations
36	 We will also allow photographer or videographer limited access the upper level
37	 Restricted Items The following items are prohibited from use in the George Roland White Studio, Founders’ Room, and other associated spaces: Helium-filled balloons Confetti, glitter, beads, or similar loose items Feather boas Sparklers or other pyrotechnic instruments Gas/wood-fueled burners (Sternos are permitted) Hay or straw Unprotected taper candles Bubbles or foam machines Champagne towers or spraying of champagne indoors Smoking is not permitted on the Premises, including the garage area
38	 Use of electronic cigarettes is also prohibited
39	 Smoking shall only be permitted at designated areas outside of the garage area as defined by Pittsburgh Opera
40	 In accordance with Pennsylvania State law, Lessee shall not permit the use of any illegal drugs in or about the Premises at any time
41	 Guest Arrival There are two large Pittsburgh Opera signs on Liberty Avenue and two A-frame signs are placed at the entrance and exit of the parking garage
42	 We do recommend that volunteers stand at the entrance and exit to the garage for your guests’ ease of mind
43	 These volunteers can answer questions about parking or the event in general
44	 VENDORS Preferred Vendors Pittsburgh Opera has no requirements about vendors, so you can feel free to use any caterer, DJ, photographer, etc
45	 that best suits your budget and taste
46	 A list of preferred Pittsburgh Opera vendors can be provided upon request
47	 Kitchen Access Pittsburgh Opera has a small kitchen that the caterer may use for prep work
48	 Because of poor ventilation, however, this is a warming kitchen and cannot accommodate cooking
49	 If your menu requires on-site cooking, most caterers will set up a kitchen in the parking garage, where ventilation is not a factor
50	 Alcohol Policy You are responsible for bringing in your own alcohol for the event, if desired
51	 Many caterers will provide alcohol and bartenders for your event, at your request
52	 Our only stipulation with the bar is that, due to PA liquor laws, you cannot sell alcohol on site, so the bar must be an open bar
53	 Announcements & Music Pittsburgh Opera will not provide A/V equipment for your event
54	 If you wish to have music or announcements made, it is recommended to hire a DJ
55	 AMENITIES Ice Machine/Ice Storage Unfortunately, we do not have an ice machine
56	 We do, however, have an ice chest located in the kitchen that can hold approximately 4-5 bags of ice
57	 You will need to provide the ice
58	 If you bring coolers with extra ice, they must be kept in the garage
59	 Guest Parking Events have use of our parking garage on a first-come, first-served basis
60	 While we will strive to ensure that at least some of your guests have use of this convenience, full use of the garage is not guaranteed
61	 If our garage is full, other options include street parking (which is metered below 25 th Street), the $10 lot at the corner of Penn Avenue and 24 th Street (1 block from the Opera), and the $6 garage at the corner of Smallman Street and 24 th Street (2 blocks from the Opera)
62	 The lot across from the Opera on Liberty Avenue is a private lot and not available for rental
63	 Many rental groups arrange for a shuttle service for their guests, though this is by no means required
64	 Two of our preferred vendors – D & P Valet and The Hub – can also provide parking services
65	 D & P Valet can be onsite to valet guests’ cars
66	 The Hub will set up an account with the Lessee to allow guest’s parking fees to be billed to the Lessee directly
67	 Pittsburgh Opera Tables and Chairs Providing our equipment isn’t being used for another event in the building, the Opera has the following: 1 10 25 6 5 10 2 5 350 72” round table (seats up to 10 people) 60” round tables (seats up to 8 people) 30” low round tables (seats up to 4 people) 26” high round tables 8’ x 24” rectangle tables 6’ x 24” rectangular utility tables 5’ bar units black leather chairs banquet chairs (maroon with gold trim) banquet chairs (maroon with gold trim) Piano Use Pittsburgh Opera usually keeps a grand piano in each room but they are removed if not being used
68	   If your group is interested in using a piano, a small piano tuning fee will be added to your final invoice
69	 Pittsburgh Opera does NOT supply a pianist, so you will be required to hire one of your own
70	 ADMINISTRATIVE Administrative Fees $150 insurance fee covers general liability for insurance for the space
71	 If you wish to purchase more coverage or are looking for additional, generic event insurance, then you may purchase additional plans
72	 $450 staffing fee covers a Pittsburgh Opera staff member to be on-site for the entirety of your rental from set-up to clean-up and a third-party security guard hired by Pittsburgh Opera
73	 PO staff will ensure building systems are working throughout and stay clean during your event, along with basic support of PO equipment
74	 $150 cleaning fee covers the additional cleaning of the event space, floors, , bathrooms, and kitchen
